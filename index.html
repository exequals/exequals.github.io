Our Mission

We started ExEquals because we saw the educational apps on the market, and thought, we can do better.

We are a purposeful team of software engineers, educators, and entrepreneurs. We created ExEquals in our senior year of high school, when we saw a lack of educational material in the app stores. As workers in tech, we hope to assist in the movement of bringing technology to the classroom. We are on a mission to change the way people learn.
